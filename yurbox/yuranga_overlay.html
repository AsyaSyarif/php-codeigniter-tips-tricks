Fits into any enterprise environment

Stash doesn’t force you to use a pre-packaged appliance you don’t have any control over – it runs on your servers. Built with infrastructure flexibility needs in mind, administrators have full control over how Stash fits into their environment including database, operating system and authentication (such as HTTPS/SSH)
